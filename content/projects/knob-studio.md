## Overview

Knob Studio is a Max for Live device that expands parameter mapping and sound modulation in Ableton Live. Designed for producers, sound designers, and musicians, it combines precise control with room for unexpected discovery — a space where careful sculpting and spontaneous ideas coexist.

At its core, Knob Studio is a mapping experimenter built around evolving waveforms and customizable functions that drive complex, layered modulation patterns.

## What it does

- **Layered modulation** — stack multiple waveform functions, each with its own adjustable properties, to build complex modulation patterns
- **Versatile reading heads** — control modulation manually, via LFO, or by following the audio envelope
- **Real-time visual feedback** — a visual interface reflects modulations as they happen, making it easier to understand and refine your sound
- **Randomization** — generate unexpected results on demand for fresh ideas and creative detours
- **Global smoothing and jitter** — dial in precision or introduce variation across all modulations at once

## Use cases

Knob Studio works across a wide range of production contexts: adding movement to synths, creating evolving effects, designing detailed soundscapes, or pushing modulation into unexpected territory. It's built to be useful whether you're working methodically or exploring intuitively.
