I'm Alessandro Miracapillo — a Berlin-based programmer and sound designer working at the intersection of audio software, web development, and creative technology. I go by Aylesim.

As an **audio developer**, I build Max/MSP instruments and Ableton Live devices: things you actually perform with. My devices ship through [Isotonik Studios](https://isotonikstudios.com/brand/aylesim/) and [my personal store](https://aylesim.gumroad.com), and have been covered by CDM and Attack Magazine.

As a **web developer**, I build tools and platforms for cultural organizations and communities. I founded [Max Berlin Network](https://www.maxberlin.network), a hub for the Max/MSP community in Berlin, and built [Planetary Compendium](https://planetarycompendium.com), an interactive reference for space exploration.

As a **creative technologist**, I develop installations, AV performances, and digital artworks — work that lives in physical spaces and responds to the people inside them. I received the Italian National Arts Award in Electronic Arts in 2022.
